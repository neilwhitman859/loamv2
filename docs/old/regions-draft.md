## Summary

**Total: ~250 regions across 62 countries**
**Parent regions: 14 (California, Oregon, Washington, New York, Central Coast, Bordeaux, Rhône Valley, Catalonia, Coastal Region, South Australia, Western Australia, New South Wales, Victoria, Wairarapa)**

## Naming Conventions
- English names used where standard in professional wine trade (Burgundy not Bourgogne, Tuscany not Toscana, Piedmont not Piemonte)
- Local names retained where that's how labels/professionals refer to them (Mosel, Pfalz, Tokaj, Barossa Valley)
- Aligned with Wine-Searcher and Decanter regional naming conventions
